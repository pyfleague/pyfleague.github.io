Welcome to the PYF Draft Hub.

In honor of the Rio Olympic Games, we will be conducting a two week long competition in order to determine draft order preferences for the 2016 season.

The Rules

We've selected four events to be tracked for the duration of the games. Prior to the medal round of each event, each league member will submit their predicted top **five** finishers for the event.

  • For each finisher correctly predicted to be in the top five, 1 point is rewarded.
  • If the fifth place finisher is correctly predicted, 5 points are rewarded. If the first place finisher is correctly predicted, 1 point is rewarded.
  • A total of 20 points can be earned for each event.
  • Draft order preference will be selected based on the total number of points at the end of the Olympic games on August 21st, 2016 - exactly one week prior to the league draft.

The Events

  • Women’s Team All-Around Gymnastics (Tuesday, August 9th)
  • Women’s 3m Springboard Diving (Saturday, August 13th 3:00pm)
  • Men's Radial Laser Dinghy Sailing (Monday, August 15th 1:05pm)
  • Draft order preference will be selected based on the total number of points at the end of the Olympic games on August 21st, 2016 - exactly one week prior to the league draft.
